# Visual Style Template

## Format Specification
FORMAT: Single 2K portrait grid (9 panel-pairs), 9:16 vertical orientation for short-form microdrama
RESOLUTION: 2K
ASPECT_RATIO: 9:16

## Rendering Style
STYLE: Photorealistic Cinematic — contemporary luxury drama, night Moscow aesthetic
CAMERA: Sony Venice 2 / Arri Alexa Mini LF (9:16 portrait crop, vertical framing)
LENS: 35mm–85mm prime lenses, shallow depth of field, soft anamorphic oval bokeh on background city lights
POST: Subtle 35mm digital film grain, deep shadow rolloff, rich skin-tone warmth, luxury-grade color grading

## Atmosphere
ATMOSPHERE: Seductive tension, ironic predatory glamour, night-city luxury, psychological cat-and-mouse, controlled desire
LIGHTING: Available-light-adjacent — dashboard amber glow, city light bleed through tinted glass, soft warm rim light on faces, cool teal fill from exterior
COLOR: Deep navy-black shadows (true black, not lifted), warm amber skin tones under interior lighting, cold teal highlights from street, gold bokeh points (city lights, cufflink glints)

## Technical
RENDER: Photorealistic 2K, sharp focus on faces and detail objects (cufflinks, fingertips, eyes), shallow DOF blurs interiors and city streaks
GRAIN: Subtle 35mm digital grain — adds filmic texture without visible noise
