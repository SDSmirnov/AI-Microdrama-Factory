## EPISODE TYPE: arc_open — FIRST EPISODE OF THE ARC UNIT

This is always the first episode of the arc unit (N=2 or N=3).
Single POV — one character's perspective throughout all episodes in the unit.

**Arc position:** panels 1–9 of the full arc.
**Dramatic function:** Establish the world, hook the viewer, build to the suspended mid-arc bridge.

**Cold Open (mandatory, P1):** hook_type: cold_open.
Consequence before cause. Reaction before stimulus. Wound before weapon.
Never exposition. Never establishing shot. Never character introduction.
Open on a fragment that demands completion — the viewer must ask "what happened?" or "what is about to happen?"

**Verbal Hook (mandatory, P2):** hook_type: verbal_hook.
The central conflict of the entire arc crystallised in ≤8 words.
This line hangs unanswered until arc_close. It is a demand, not an explanation.

**Arc Bridge (mandatory, P9):** hook_type: arc_bridge.
- Physical suspension: the action is frozen mid-motion at the threshold but not over it.
- sound_design: silence (always — the episode boundary is a sonic reset).
- motion_prompt ends before the action completes.
- visual_end must contain a match_cut shape that connects to the next episode's arc_pickup visual_start.
- NOT a cliffhanger — the dramatic peak is in arc_close, not here.

**Motif seeding (mandatory):**
Establish one visual motif (object, gesture, framing, or color) in this episode.
Tag in visual_continuity_rules as "MOTIF: [description]."
It will be called back in arc_close.p9 (cliffhanger) — same framing, transformed meaning.
In N=3 arcs, it will echo briefly in arc_mid without payoff.

**Continuity forward:** All state changes (location, costume, prop, injury) must be listed in visual_continuity_rules for the next episode to maintain.
