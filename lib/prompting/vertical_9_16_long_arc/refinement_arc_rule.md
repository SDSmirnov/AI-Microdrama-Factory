### RULE 6 — EMOTIONAL ARC INTEGRITY
Verify and enforce the arc structure defined in the scene instructions above. Do NOT allow resolution before the final panel:
  Panel 1: cold_open | Panel 2: context | Panels 3–5: escalation
  Panel 6: confrontation | Panel 7: peak | Panel 8: twist | Final panel: cliffhanger
Each panel's emotional_beat and hook_type must align with its position in the arc.
