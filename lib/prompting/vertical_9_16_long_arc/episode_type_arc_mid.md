## EPISODE TYPE: arc_mid — MIDDLE EPISODE(S) OF THE ARC UNIT (N≥3)

This episode type fills all middle slots between arc_open and arc_close. N=3: one arc_mid. N=4: two arc_mids. N=5: three arc_mids.
Single POV — same character perspective as arc_open.

**Arc position (per N):**
- N=3: panels 10–18 of 27 (Ep2, labeled locally P1–9)
- N=4: panels 10–18 of 36 (Ep2) or panels 19–27 of 36 (Ep3)
- N=5: panels 10–18 of 45 (Ep2), 19–27 (Ep3), or 28–36 (Ep4)

**Dramatic function:** Escalate beyond all prior episodes, introduce new complications, and ratchet pressure toward arc_close's confrontation. Each successive arc_mid must feel more urgent than the last — the gap between them must never be neutral.

**Mandatory new element (each arc_mid independently):** Every arc_mid must introduce at least one element not present in any prior episode of the arc:
- A new revelation that reframes what the viewer has already seen.
- A new obstacle, character, or location that forces a different path.
- A new piece of information that makes arc_close's confrontation inevitable.
Without this, the arc_mid is filler. Filler kills pacing.

**Escalation rule for multiple arc_mids (N=4 and N=5):** The second arc_mid (Ep3) must feel structurally closer to confrontation than the first (Ep2). By its P8, physical convergence must be visible. The third arc_mid (N=5, Ep4) is the last breath before the collision — its P8 must be the final approach, not a complication.

**Arc Pickup (mandatory, P1):** hook_type: arc_pickup.
Same location, same moment as arc_open's arc_bridge, 1–2 seconds later.
The action from the bridge now completes — the decision crosses the threshold.
A viewer arriving here first must read the stakes through visible action, not exposition.

**Pre-Confrontation (mandatory, P8):**
By P8, the collision between forces must be physically inevitable — characters converging, distance closing.
This is not the confrontation itself (that belongs to arc_close.p2–p4); it is the final beat before it.
State in screenplay_instructions: what physical motion or positioning signals the approach.

**Arc Bridge (mandatory, P9):** hook_type: arc_bridge.
- Same mechanics as arc_open: physical suspension, sound_design=silence, motion_prompt ends before completion.
- visual_end must contain a match_cut shape connecting to arc_close's arc_pickup visual_start.
- The bridge must feel more urgent than the previous episode's bridge — the stakes are higher, the threshold closer to violence or collapse. Each successive arc_mid bridge should feel like a tighter spring than the last.

**Motif echo (mandatory):** The visual motif from arc_open must appear briefly in this episode (not as payoff — as recognition). Note in visual_continuity_rules: "MOTIF ECHO: [motif] — [where it appears, briefly, without transformation]."

**Continuity:** All state changes must propagate to arc_close's visual_continuity_rules.
