# Visual Style: Vertical Dark Romance

## Format Specification
FORMAT: Single 2K portrait grid (9 panel-pairs), 9:16 vertical orientation for short-form vertical romance series
RESOLUTION: 2K
ASPECT_RATIO: 9:16

## Rendering Style
STYLE: Photorealistic Cinematic — dark romance, luxury tension, emotional intimacy
CAMERA: Sony Venice 2 / Arri Alexa Mini LF (9:16 portrait crop, vertical framing)
LENS: 50mm–85mm prime lenses, shallow depth of field. Tight proximity framing — camera always closer than socially comfortable.
POST: Subtle 35mm digital film grain, deep shadow rolloff, warm amber-burgundy midtones, luxury color grading with deliberate cool-warm split

## Atmosphere
ATMOSPHERE: Forbidden desire, controlled obsession, emotional tension barely contained beneath a composed surface
LIGHTING: Single practical source dominant — candlelight, floor lamp, city-window spill, phone screen glow. Shadows always present on one side of the face. No flat lighting — darkness is narrative.
COLOR PALETTE: Deep jewel tones — midnight blue, burgundy, charcoal, gold. Skin tones warm amber. Blacks are true black. One deliberate accent: a red detail, a gold object, a pale hand against dark fabric.

## Technical
RENDER: Photorealistic 2K, maximum facial detail at CU and ECU — pores, micro-expressions, the slight tension of a controlled jaw. Shallow DOF isolates faces from luxury interiors.
GRAIN: Subtle 35mm digital grain — adds heat and texture, suggests intimacy.
BOKEH: Warm, oval bokeh from practical light sources. Background elements (candles, city lights, chandeliers) dissolve into soft gold circles behind the subject's face.

## Character Appearance Standard
- Faces: camera-ready but real — dewy skin, sharp catchlight, no AI smoothing
- Male lead: controlled elegance — luxury clothing, no visible effort, dangerous stillness
- Female lead: composed intelligence — understated beauty, the kind that increases under scrutiny
- Expression baseline: composed surfaces with visible subtext — the held jaw, the eyes that track without moving the head
