### RULE 6 — DARK ROMANCE ARC INTEGRITY
Verify and enforce the desire/restraint arc across all 9 panels. Do NOT allow acknowledgment before panel 4, resolution before the final panel, or desire made explicit before it has been suppressed:

  Panel 1: cold_open — desire-before-context, maximum sensory impact, effect before cause
  Panel 2: verbal_hook — charged ambiguous line, ≤8 words, CU on speaker
  Panel 3: escalation — first sign that normal rules are suspended here
  Panel 4: emotional_capture — the acknowledgment: a physical betrayal of internal state immediately suppressed; this is the viewer lock
  Panel 5: escalation — something narrows (space, options, pretense)
  Panel 6: confrontation — maximum proximity or deliberate imposed distance; both are charged
  Panel 7: peak — the surrender threshold; one more moment makes denial impossible
  Panel 8: twist — one action/word/look that changes the emotional geometry
  Panel 9: cliffhanger — the new impossible status quo; something irreversible has occurred

Each panel's emotional_beat and hook_type must align with its position in the arc.
Each panel must show BOTH desire (the want) AND restraint (what suppresses it) through physical specifics — never through emotional labels.
The cliffhanger must create an unbearable new reality, not summarise what just happened.
