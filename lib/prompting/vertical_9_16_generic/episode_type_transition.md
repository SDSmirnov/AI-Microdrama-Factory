## EPISODE TYPE: TRANSITION — TIME GAP OR LOCATION BRIDGE
This episode bridges a time gap or location change between scenes. It is primarily visual and sonic — minimal narrative action.
- ALL panels: panel_type = "narrative", duration 2–4s. Environmental and location shots preferred over character close-ups.
- dialogue: "" (empty) for ALL panels unless the source has dialogue in transit.
- voiceover: brief inner monologue or narration only if the source text has it during this passage.
- VISUAL CONTINUITY: show the connecting tissue between scenes — travel, passage of time, change of environment.
- transition_to_next: prefer match_cut between panels (matching geometric shape or motion vector). smash_cut for the final panel into the next scene.
- Sound design carries atmosphere: ambient sound, silence, environmental audio. Reflect the source text's mood during this passage.
- The episode communicates the passage of time or space through visual and sonic texture.
