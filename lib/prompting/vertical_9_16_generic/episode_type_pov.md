## EPISODE TYPE: POV — SINGLE CHARACTER PERSPECTIVE (INTERIOR)
This episode shows events from one character's point of view — their actions, interior thoughts, and immediate observations.
- SINGLE POV THROUGHOUT: only this character's experience is depicted. Supporting characters are present but never given equal screen weight — they are part of the environment that the protagonist navigates.
- Follow the source text's emotional register for this character. If the source shows them calm, don't force agitation. If the source shows confusion, show confusion.
- EPISODE ENDING: end where the source text ends — quietly, on a charged moment, or mid-thought. Do not add escalation.
